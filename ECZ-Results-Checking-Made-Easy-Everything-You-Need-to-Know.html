ECZ Results Checking Made Easy: Everything You Need to Know

January 17, 2025 | ECZ Solutions Team | 6 min read

You can now easily check your ECZ results by following the steps outlined below. The Examinations Council of Zambia (ECZ) is responsible for administering examinations in Zambia, and there are several ways to access your results.

How to Check ECZ Results Using SMS

Candidates can check their results by sending an SMS to 8383. The message format is:

Examination-number year-of-examination Grade

To check ECZ results via SMS, follow these steps:

  1. Open your SMS app on your mobile phone.
  2. Type the following message:
    Examination-number year-of-examination Grade
    For example: 12345678 2022 G12
  3. Send the message to 8383 using an MTN, Airtel, or Zamtel SIM card.
  4. You will receive a reply with your results.
Example: If a candidate's examination number is 12345678 and they want to check the results for the 2022 Grade 12 examination, they would send the SMS 12345678 2022 G12 to 8383.
Important Note: Please note that there will be a charge for sending this SMS.

How to Check ECZ Results Online

Candidates can also check their results online by visiting the ECZ website and clicking on the "E-Statement of Results" link. They will need to enter their examination number and the year of the examination.

To check ECZ results online, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the ECZ website at the official Examinations Council of Zambia portal.
  2. Click on the "E-Statement of Results" link in the main navigation menu.
  3. Select the level for which you want to check results from the dropdown menu (e.g., Grade 12, Grade 9, Grade 7).
  4. Enter your examination number in the provided textbox.
  5. Click on "Continue" to proceed.
  6. Your results will be displayed after you click continue.
  7. You can print your results by clicking on the "Print Statement of Results" button for your records.

Alternative Method: Checking Results at School

Candidates can also check their results at their school, where a copy of the statement of results will be available. This is particularly useful for students who may not have immediate access to mobile phones or internet services.

Final Note: Checking ECZ results is an important step in your educational journey. Whether you choose to use SMS, online methods, or visit your school, ensure you have your examination number ready for a smooth process.
